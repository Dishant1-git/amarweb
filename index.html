Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about windows and doors

Our goal is to provide transparent information and expert guidance so you can feel confident in selecting the best solutions for your project. If you need more details, our team is always ready to assist you.

Call Us

1. What types of windows and doors do you offer?

We offer a wide range of premium uPVC and Aluminium windows and doors, including casement windows, sliding sash windows, bifolding doors, sliding patio doors, and composite front doors, all custom-made to your specifications.

2. Do you provide custom size solutions?

Yes, all of our glazing products are manufactured to your exact measurements. Our surveyor will visit your property to take precise measurements to ensure a perfect fit and high-quality seal.

3. How long does installation take?

Installation time depends on the project size, but most residential installations are completed within one to three days. We make sure to clean up after ourselves and leave your home spotless.

4. Do you provide repair and maintenance services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive double-glazing repairs, glass replacements, hinge and handle replacements, and general window and door maintenance services.

5. Are your windows and doors energy efficient?

Absolutely. All of our glazing options utilize advanced thermal insulation technologies, double or triple glazing, and low-emissivity coatings to achieve high energy-efficiency ratings and reduce your heating bills.
